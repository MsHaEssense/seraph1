History of Owari no Seraph



Among these survivors are Yuuichirou and Mikaela Hyakuya, two young boys who are taken captive from an orphanage, along with other children whom they consider family. Discontent with being treated like livestock under the vampires' cruel reign, Mikaela hatches a rebellious escape plan that is ultimately doomed to fail.


The only survivor to come out on the other side is Yuuichirou, who is found by the Moon Demon Company, a military unit dedicated to exterminating the vampires in Japan. Yūichirō, whose family was slaughtered by vampires, joins a military organization called the Moon Demon Company, which specializes in combating the vampires. His primary goal is to avenge his family and protect the remaining humans from the vampire tyranny. Alongside him are other soldiers and comrades who each have their own motivations for joining the fight against the vampires.




Season 1

Vampire Reign introduces a world devastated by a vampire apocalypse, following Yūichirō Hyakuya's quest for revenge.






Season 2

Battle in Nagoya escalates the conflict as Yūichirō and his comrades face off against powerful vampire forces.





Season 3

Battle in the Holy City delves deeper into the supernatural powers at play, as the war between humans and vampires intensifies.
