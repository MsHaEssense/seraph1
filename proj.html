The Sacred Projects


1. Seraph of the End Project (終わりのセラフ計画)

A human experimentation project conducted by both the Japanese Imperial Demon Army and the vampires. It involves using cursed experiments and angelic powers to turn humans into powerful beings, essentially weapons.

2. Hyakuya Sect Experiments

A secretive cult that experimented on orphans, including Yūichirō and Mikaela. They tried to create artificial Seraphs, leading to unnatural abilities in children. The sect worked closely with both Guren Ichinose and the Hīragi family, aiming to control humanity's fate.

3. Demon Army’s Cursed Gear Project

A project led by Guren Ichinose to create weapons infused with demons. Allows soldiers to wield immense power, but at the risk of being possessed by their demons. Shinoa’s squad and other Moon Demon Company members use these weapons.

4. Vampire’s Human Livestock System

The vampire nobles created a system where humans are kept as "livestock" for blood. They run experiments on some humans to enhance their abilities. This system ensures the vampires have a constant food supply while controlling human populations.
